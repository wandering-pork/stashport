## üéØ Product North Star

**Stashport exists to help users:**

> *Plan a trip, feel proud of it, and easily share it as inspiration or social content.*

Any feature included in MVP must clearly support at least one of the following:
- Faster or easier trip creation
- Making trips feel publishable and complete
- Encouraging sharing, reuse, or inspiration

If a feature does **not** support one of these outcomes, it is **not MVP**.

## 1Ô∏è‚É£ First-Time User Success (P0 / P1)

### Story 1: Post-Signup Confirmation Clarity (P0)
**As a new user**, I want clear instructions after signup so I can access my account without confusion.

**Acceptance Criteria**
- Dedicated confirmation page or login banner
- Displays registered email
- Resend confirmation email option
- Clear guidance (check spam, timing expectations)

---

### Story 2: Guided Onboarding Flow (P1)
**As a new user**, I want guidance after signing up so I know what to do next.

**Includes**
- Step 1: Travel style (Solo, Couple, Family, Group)
- Step 2: Intent (Plan a trip / Browse inspiration / Share past trip)
- Step 3: CTA (Create first trip or Explore featured)
- Fully skippable, under 30 seconds

## üöÄ MVP Success Definition

Stashport MVP is successful when:
- Users complete a first trip without friction

- Users actively copy captions or share links
